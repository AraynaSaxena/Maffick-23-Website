Welcome to the Central India"s Largest
Techno-cultural Fest

Maffick, the annual techno-cultural fest of NIT Bhopal and largest fests in central India, is a boisterous festival brimming with sensational events and perpetual fun. It is an extravagant platform to bring forth the artist inside you with its broad-gauging competitions. From showcasing your thunderous dance moves to unveiling your impeccable fashion sense, Maffick lets you do it all. Be a witness to a slurry of electrifying events and huge surge of exuberance.

PANACHE

Group Fashion Show

Theme: Tribal/Ethnic/Traditional

Among the most anticipated parts of Maffick '23 is Panache, a group fashion show. Featuring fashion enthusiasts from all over the country, ‘Panache’ is an acclamation of fashion, fused with creativity and originality. In this edition, ethnic Indian attires will be given a contemporary spin. In addition to a magnificent runway, splendid decor, and fine lighting, Panache sets the stage for an action-packed evening that's sure to enchant. Spectators are bound to be captivated as they watch each squad walk down the runway with poise and grace, showcasing the intricate details and unique features of their outfits. Each outfit adorned by the participants is a reflection of their individuality and ingenuity.
Catch us for a memorable experience on the 5th of March, 6:30pm onwards!

Body Rock

Dance Competition

Bodyrock, a highly anticipated dance competition, is a part of Maffick'23. It draws in talented dancers who are eager to showcase their skills and compete for the top prize. Whether you want to fly solo or dance with a partner, the stage is yours to command. From the slick moves of hip-hop to the fluid grace of contemporary, every dance style is on the table, allowing you to express yourself in your own unique way. Experience this vibrant energy of arts and get ready to be swept away by the magic of dance.

SWARANGAN

Singing Competition

Swarangan; the ultimate singing showdown, is here to give you a chance to take the mic and use your voice to create magic. Swarangan is a platform for vocalists of different ages and socioeconomic levels to compete against one another, initiated by Maffick. It gives you the chance to perform in front of a live audience and be judged by a panel of music judges, whether you're a solo artist or part of the group. Ultimately, this is a great chance for singers of all skill levels to demonstrate their skills, receive exposure, and take one more step further towards music.
Join us on the 6th March, 10pm onwards at the civil auditorium, MANIT campus.
